Kartoshka

Kartoshka, or Soviet Potato Cakes, are a dessert created during the Soviet era as a way to use up old pastries at bakeries. Today, kartoshka are made with store bought cookies and are extremely easy to make at home.

Ingredients:

  • Butter 100 grams
  • Sweetened condensed milk 200 grams
  • Cookies, any kind, 300 grams
  • Cocoa powder, 1 tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Soften butter until it's very soft but not melted
  2. Add the condensed milk to the butter and mix to combine.
  3. Crush the cookies into crumbs (can use a food processor). Add cookie crumbs to butter and condensed mixture and mix.
  4. Shape in balls. You can also shape them to look like little potatoes to fit the name.
  5. Once all your balls/potatoes are shaped, roll them in the cocoa powder to cover them and to make them look even more potato like.
  6. Refrigerate for at least an hour or two and enjoy!
